Who we are

S.L.U.T.S. is a volunteer group focused on one thing: taking responsibility for the places we live. No big speeches. No complicated rules. Just showing up with gloves and bags and doing the work.

Low ego

Anyone can help. Come as you are. Do what you can.

High follow-through

We pick a time, pick a place, and we go.

Local impact

Parks, trails, streets — the places we all share.

What we do

  • Organize small-to-medium community trash cleanups
  • Coordinate supplies (bags, grabbers, gloves) when possible
  • Share meetup info and before/after results
  • Make it easy for new people to join without pressure
Safety note: We keep it common-sense: daylight hours, public areas, and no heroics.
